Carnation City Mall Site Redesign

The Carnation City Mall website was not supplying critical information needed by its users. A study was undertaken to determine how the site could be of better use to their shoppers.

This project was completed as a proof-of-concept for a fictional client in order to complete the requirements for a graduate course.

I was responsible for the following in this project:

  • Completing a competitive analysis
  • Developing a redesigned blueprint for the mall's website
  • Analysis and documentation of information to provide a recommendation for new site design
